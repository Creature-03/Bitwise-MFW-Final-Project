Final Fantasy IX is a role-playing video game developed and published by Square for the PlayStation video game console. It is the ninth game in the main Final Fantasy series.
Game development occurred in parallel with Final Fantasy VIII. Envisioned by developers as a retrospective for the series, it departed from the futuristic settings of Final Fantasy VI, Final Fantasy VII, and Final Fantasy VIII by returning to the medieval style of the earlier games. Consequently, it draws heavy influence from the original Final Fantasy and features allusions to the rest of the series. The game introduced new features to the series despite this approach, such as "Active Time Event" cutscenes, "Mognet", and skill systems. Final Fantasy IX was the last game in the main series whose music was composed solely by Nobuo Uematsu.
Final Fantasy IX was released in the year 2000 to critical acclaim and is considered one of the greatest video games of all time. Often cited by critics and fans as one of the best Final Fantasy games, it also holds the highest Metacritic score of the series. Final Fantasy IX was commercially successful, selling more than 5.5 million copies on PlayStation by March 2016. Square Enix released ports featuring minor gameplay and graphical enhancements for various other platforms in the late 2010s.

Queen Brahne Raza Alexandros XVI of the kingdom of Alexandria lusts for power, and seeks to increase her domain by conquering the surrounding lands. Regent Cid of Lindblum worries how this will impact Princess Garnet, and dispatches the Tantalus Theater Troupe to kidnap her.
The princess yearns to escape from her wicked mother and willingly boards the Theater Ship. During the operation Vivi, a lone black mage, and Adelbert Steiner, Captain of the Royal Knights of Pluto, become involved. During their escape the Theater Ship is shot down and crashes into the Evil Forest. Brahne is outraged Garnet escaped with a valuable crystal, a royal heirloom, and dispatches the Black Waltzes to retrieve her.

Along their journey each character will be faced with life changing events, difficult to face questions, and even more difficult to accept answers. The story touches on themes of death, betrayal, purpose, love, abandonment, and many more very real topics. Although even throughout the heavy themes of the story, the game finds an appropriate balance of serious moments, light-hearted commedy, and beating the snot out of monsters such that the game is extremely enjoyable to play despite the sad undertone of the characters stories.
